# Cover Letter Draft for Information Sciences

Dear Editor,

We submit "CODA-SC: Calibrated Selective Edge-Cloud Inference Under Internet
Deadlines" for consideration as an original research article in Information
Sciences.

The manuscript contributes a general computational-intelligence framework for
reliable inference when intelligent systems must process information across
resource-variable device, edge, and cloud environments. CODA-SC combines
conformal or conformal-risk-control early exits with an online deadline-aware
split controller for uncertain samples. This yields a balanced methodological
and empirical contribution: finite-sample reliability analysis, online
constraint-control analysis, ablations, calibration and action-mix diagnostics,
and reproducible experiments across multiple vision workloads and
Internet-like network profiles.

The work fits Information Sciences because it advances machine learning,
uncertainty-aware decision making, and intelligent systems methodology rather
than applying a known method to a single domain. The submitted package includes
reproducible code, fixed seeds, generated result tables, figures, highlights,
and a graphical abstract.

This manuscript is original, is not under consideration elsewhere, and all
authors have approved the submission.

Sincerely,

The Authors
